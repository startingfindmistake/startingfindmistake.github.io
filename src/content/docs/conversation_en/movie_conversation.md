what genre of movies do you like the most?
Please also tell me if there is a particular reason why you like it.

* genre = 장르


I like comedy and action genres.

I like comedy because it allows me to forget the hardships of daily life 
and the problems I have to solve in the real world, 
even if only for a while.


I also like action movies because they make the impossible possible, giving me motivation and energy.


* hardships = 풍파
* even if only for a while = 잠시나마 (비록 짧은 기간일지라도)
* motivation = 동기부여
* giving me = 얻다



왜냐하면 코미디는 저한테 현실세계에서의 힘든 일상이나 풀어야할 문제들을 잠시나마 잊게 만들어줘서 코미디를 좋아합니다.


또한 액션 영화는 불가능한것을 가능하게 만들어 저한테 동기부여 및 에너지를 얻을수 있어서 좋아합니다.

Who is your favorite actor?
Which movie starring that actor left the deepest impression on you?


* starring = 주연
* deepest impression = 감명 깊은
* left deepest impression = 가장 깊은 인상을 남겼다.
* on you = 너한테
